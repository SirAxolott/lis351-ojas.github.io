Heroes of Camp Half-Blood

Demigods, also known as half-bloods, are the children of Greek gods and mortal humans. They possess unique abilities inherited from their godly parents and face the challenges of straddling both the mortal and mythological worlds. Many find refuge at Camp Half-Blood, where they train to become heroes.

The Main Heroes

These extraordinary demigods have saved the world multiple times and played pivotal roles in both the Titan and Giant Wars.

Percy Jackson

Son of Poseidon • Hero of Olympus

The protagonist of the series, Percy discovers he's a demigod at age twelve. Known for his loyalty, bravery, and wit, he's one of the most powerful demigods of his generation. His control over water and natural leadership abilities have helped save both Camp Half-Blood and Olympus multiple times.

  • Powers Hydrokinesis, Healing in Water, Communication with Sea Life
  • Weapon Riptide (Anaklusmos)
  • Notable Achievements Retrieved Zeus's Master Bolt, Held up the Sky, Led the Defense of Olympus

Annabeth Chase

Daughter of Athena • Architect of Olympus

A brilliant strategist and architect, Annabeth has been at Camp Half-Blood since she was seven. Her intelligence and courage have been crucial in countless quests. She's known for her deep knowledge of mythology and her dream of creating something permanent.

  • Skills Strategy, Architecture, Combat
  • Weapon Celestial Bronze Dagger, Yankees Cap of Invisibility
  • Notable Achievements Retrieved the Athena Parthenos, Redesigned Olympus

Grover Underwood

Lord of the Wild • Chosen One of Pan

Though technically not a demigod, Grover is a satyr and one of the main heroes. He's Percy's best friend and protector, known for his bravery despite his fears. His connection to nature and dedication to protecting the wild make him a crucial ally.

  • Powers Nature Magic, Empathy Link, Woodland Magic
  • Weapon Reed Pipes, Nature Magic
  • Notable Achievements Found Pan, Created the Council of Cloven Elders

Other Notable Heroes

Luke Castellan

Son of Hermes • The Fallen Hero

Once the head counselor of the Hermes cabin, Luke's bitterness towards the gods led him down a dark path. His story is one of redemption, ultimately sacrificing himself to stop Kronos and save Olympus.

  • Skills: Swordsmanship, Thievery, Leadership
  • Weapon: Backbiter/Kronos's Scythe
  • Legacy: His sacrifice helped bridge the gap between gods and their children

Thalia Grace

Daughter of Zeus • Lieutenant of Artemis

After being turned into a tree protecting Camp Half-Blood, Thalia returned to life and eventually joined the Hunters of Artemis. Her strong leadership and combat skills make her a formidable ally.

  • Powers: Electrokinesis, Control over Air
  • Weapon: Spear, Aegis Shield
  • Role: Lieutenant of the Hunters of Artemis

Nico di Angelo

Son of Hades • Ghost King

Initially introduced as a cheerful young boy, Nico's journey through loss and acceptance transformed him into a powerful and respected hero. His control over the dead and shadows makes him uniquely powerful.

  • Powers: Necromancy, Shadow Travel, Geokinesis
  • Weapon: Stygian Iron Sword
  • Notable Achievements: Convinced Hades to join the Olympian Battle

Clarisse La Rue

Daughter of Ares • Drakon Slayer

A fierce warrior and leader, Clarisse proves that there's more to being a child of Ares than just fighting. Her bravery and leadership during the Battle of Manhattan earned her great respect.

  • Skills: Combat, Strategy, Leadership
  • Weapon: Electric Spear, "Lamer"
  • Notable Achievement: Slew the Lydian Drakon

Rachel Elizabeth Dare

Oracle of Delphi • Clear-Sighted Mortal

Though not a demigod, Rachel's ability to see through the Mist and her role as the Oracle make her an essential part of the demigod world. Her prophecies guide the heroes on their quests.

  • Powers: Clear Sight, Prophecy
  • Role: Oracle of Delphi
  • Notable Achievement: Guided Heroes through the Labyrinth

Piper McLean

Daughter of Aphrodite • Charmspeaker

Breaking the stereotypes of Aphrodite's children, Piper proves that love can be a powerful force in battle. Her charmspeak ability and bravery make her a formidable hero.

  • Powers: Charmspeak, Beauty Manipulation
  • Weapon: Katoptris
  • Notable Achievement: Defeated Khione, Goddess of Snow
